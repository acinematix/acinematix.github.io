Home About Portrait Landscape Street Portfolio

Street and portrait work from India, shot over multiple trips. My mother tongue opens the door — it breaks the barrier between me and the people I photograph, and lets their stories come through. What's left is conversation, color, texture, and the quiet beauty of everyday life. The country moves fast — loud, layered, relentless. I look to slow down and sit inside it, catching the moments that live between the chaos.

Eventually, this collection will live as a photo book — for now, it grows here.
